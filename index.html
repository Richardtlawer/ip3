Rules: this is a web application that takes a number from a user and returns a range of numbers from 1 to the chosen number with the following exceptions:

Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with "ping"

Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with "pong"

Numbers divisible by 15 are replaced with "pingpong"

A user enters a new number and sees new results over and over again.
